# Reference video analysis

The supplied recording uses a distinctive interaction grammar:

1. White floating navigation over a full-bleed hero.
2. Deep green and acid-lime brand contrast.
3. Branded full-screen loader with a simple curved mark.
4. Large rounded sections and high-contrast typographic scale.
5. Alternating cream, lavender, mint, coral and green stages.
6. Large editorial cards rather than small UI cards.
7. Frequent mask, wipe, scale and parallax transitions.
8. A calculator as an interactive conversion section.
9. Testimonial, FAQ and oversized dark footer close the journey.

This project reproduces that structure and pacing without copying the reference brand, text, images or proprietary assets.
