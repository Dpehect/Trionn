# Softbridge content architecture

## Content principle
Every major page should answer six questions:
1. What problem is this for?
2. When is it useful?
3. What does Softbridge do?
4. What is delivered?
5. What risk is reduced?
6. What evidence or commercial commitment is required next?

## Page roles
- Homepage: positioning, operating model, proof, engagement entry points.
- Services: capability depth, signals, deliverables, risks, team shape and timing.
- Work: disclosure standard and evidence-based case narratives.
- Approach: operating model, principles, delivery outputs and commercial transparency.
- Careers: honest distinction between active hiring and the talent network.
- Contact: qualification, first-conversation expectations and next steps.
