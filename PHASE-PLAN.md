# 14-phase delivery plan

1. **Foundation** — Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind, routing, metadata, tokens and project structure.
2. **Product narrative** — Real Trionn positioning, hero, product value and conversion architecture.
3. **Design system** — Editorial grid, responsive typography, shadcn/Radix primitives and UI states.
4. **GSAP motion core** — @gsap/react lifecycle, CustomEase, reveal system and performance cleanup.
5. **GSAP Flip lab** — Live Signal/System/Launch interface layout transformations.
6. **Scroll storytelling** — ScrollTrigger pin, scrub, progress and product-state narrative.
7. **Split typography** — SplitText hero, masked lines and ScrollTo-controlled navigation.
8. **Observer tour** — Wheel, touch and pointer controlled product scenes with accessible controls.
9. **Draggable workflow** — Draggable + Inertia product-loop rail with bounds and snap.
10. **SVG system map** — MorphSVG and MotionPath context-flow visualisation without WebGL.
11. **Conversion system** — Zustand, Vaul, React Hook Form, Zod and Sonner request flow.
12. **Proof and pricing** — Embla stories, pricing architecture and product credibility.
13. **Production hardening** — Accessibility, reduced motion, responsive fallbacks, SEO and legal pages.
14. **Final Awwwards polish** — Timing, composition, route reveal, browser QA and production packaging.
